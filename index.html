Our Story & Mission

The Taste of North Bengal was founded in 2015 to bring the authentic flavors of North Bengal to food lovers. Our mission is to preserve centuries-old recipes while adding a modern twist in presentation, offering a dining experience that transports you to the lush landscapes, warm kitchens, and rich traditions of North Bengal.
